## How This Document Is Assembled

Each `# LEVEL N` section is synthesized from its corresponding research file (`analysis/level-4-market.md`, `analysis/level-3-customer.md`, `analysis/level-2-competitive.md`, `analysis/level-1-company.md`). Every level file follows the same **contract sections**, which map into this document as:

- **Key Findings** — 3-6 bullets, each ending with `(Source: <citation>, CRAAP: <score>/25)`
- **Detail** — the structured deep-dive (use the scaffolding provided under each level below)
- **Sources** — recorded centrally in `source-bibliography.md`
- **Confidence & Gaps** — what is solid, what is thin, what still needs triangulation

Fill the Key Findings and Confidence & Gaps blocks at the top of each level first (mechanical merge from the level file), then complete the Detail scaffolding.
